## Included Scope

WHYLD research includes:
- Conceptual and architectural research
- Protocol and framework design
- Reference implementations and models
- Stress testing and evaluation methodologies
- Governance and safety analysis

## Excluded Scope

WHYLD does not:
- Deploy production systems
- Offer hosted services
- Collect or store user data
- Perform surveillance or monitoring
- Replace regulatory or legal authority

WHYLD research is descriptive and architectural in nature.  
Implementation and deployment decisions remain the responsibility of adopting organizations.
